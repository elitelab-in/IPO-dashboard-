Who We Are

Elitelab.in was born out of a simple frustration: discovering high-probability swing trade setups in the vast Indian stock market was too time-consuming, and existing tools were either overly complicated or prohibitively expensive.

We are a dedicated team of market enthusiasts, data engineers, and algorithmic traders who decided to build the solution we always wanted. By combining live data feeds with proprietary filtering algorithms—like our signature AI auto screener for intraday and Swing trading—we cut through the noise to deliver actionable insights directly to your dashboard.

What We Do

Elitelab gives you a complete suite of professional tools to trade smarter in the Indian stock market:

AI Screener Pro

Our algorithm scans thousands of NSE & BSE stocks in real-time and automatically filters the best Intraday and Swing trade setups for you, so you never miss a high-probability entry.

Stocks Search Engine

Search any stock and instantly get deep fundamental analysis including quarterly results, profit trends, shareholding patterns, peer comparisons, and key financial ratios — all in one clean dashboard.

Macroeconomic Event Analyzer

Search real-world events like "RBI rate cut" or "crude oil crash" and see exactly which sectors and stocks get impacted, powered by live commodity feeds and an advanced correlation engine.

Sector Analysis Pro

Visualize sector rotation dynamics using live Relative Strength (RRG) scorecards, advances/declines market breadth statistics, and fortnightly institutional FPI flow tables directly from NSDL.
