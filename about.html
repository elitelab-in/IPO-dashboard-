Who We Are

Elitelab was born out of a simple frustration: discovering high-probability swing trade setups in the vast Indian stock market was too time-consuming, and existing tools were either overly complicated or prohibitively expensive.

We are a dedicated team of market enthusiasts, data engineers, and algorithmic traders who decided to build the solution we always wanted. By combining live data feeds with proprietary filtering algorithms—like our signature IPO Base screener—we cut through the noise to deliver actionable insights directly to your dashboard.

What We Do

We focus on one core philosophy: Quality over Quantity. Our algorithms scan thousands of equities across the NSE and BSE to identify unique setups, specifically focusing on newly listed companies forming solid bases. We aim to help you catch the next big breakout before it hits mainstream news.
